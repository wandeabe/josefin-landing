Common questions

Before you ask.

Is my email data private?+
Josefin reads only the messages you connect. Your data is never sold, never used for training, and you can disconnect at any time.
Do I need to download anything?+
No. Josefin works through your existing email, calendar and WhatsApp. Everything happens where you already are - no new app required.
How does my nanny use it?+
They don't need to install anything. Josefin sends them WhatsApp or SMS messages with exactly what they need to know, when they need to know it.
Is it free to join?+
Yes - the waitlist is free, and early access is free too. You get the service; we get your honest feedback. No commitment required.
Why can't I just use Claude or ChatGPT to do this myself?+
It's a fair question - especially as Claude, ChatGPT and others now offer agents, memory and connectors that can read email or browse the web. But even with those tools, you're still the one who has to set them up, maintain the connections, and remember to check in. Josefin is built from the ground up around your household: it knows your children, your nanny, your calendar, and the rhythm of your family. It acts without being asked, coordinates across multiple people, and tailors each message to who's receiving it. General-purpose AI is a powerful tool; Josefin is a service designed specifically for this job.
What email providers do you support?+
Gmail and Outlook are fully supported today. Apple Mail and other providers are on the roadmap. You can also simply forward emails to a Josefin address.
Can multiple parents use the same household?+
Yes - Josefin is designed for the whole household. Both parents can be connected, and Josefin coordinates messages between them, the nanny, grandparents, and anyone else in your circle.
