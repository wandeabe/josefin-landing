Common questions

Before you ask.

Is my email data private? +
Josefin reads only the messages you connect. Your data is never sold, never used for training, and you can disconnect at any time.
How would each person in the household use it? +
Differently, depending on the role. Anyone in the household can connect their own inbox and calendar, and Josefin coordinates across them as one. Anyone else who needs to be in the loop simply receives what concerns them through WhatsApp. No inbox access, no app to install, no setup on their end. Each person sees only what they need to, so nothing falls between people who each thought the other person was picking it up.
Why can’t I just use ChatGPT or Claude for this myself? +
General-purpose AI is a powerful tool. But you’re still the one setting it up, maintaining the connections, and remembering to check in. Josefin is built around your household: it knows the children, the nanny, the calendar, the rhythm of the week. It acts without being asked, coordinates across people, and tailors each message to who’s receiving it. A tool is something you use. Josefin is something that runs.
What email providers are supported? +
Gmail and Outlook today. Apple Mail and others are on the roadmap. You can also simply forward emails to a Josefin address.
